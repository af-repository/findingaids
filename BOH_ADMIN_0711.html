Scope and Contents

Contains records pertaining to Board of Health activities, finances, personnel, and regulatory duties. Records include burial permits and annual reports.

Return to Table of Contents »

Arrangement

Records are arranged in twelve sections: 1. Activities, 2. Brochure, 3. Burial Permits, 4. Correspondence, 5. Financial Records, 6. Insurance Claims, 7. Nurse’s Reports, 8. Personnel Records, 9. Publications (non-BOH), 10. Resources, 11. Reports, 12. Rules and Regulations.

Collection Inventory

Activities, 1947-2002 

Arrangement

Records are arranged chronologically.

Scope and Contents

Contains Board of Health outreach material, professional development material, and health resources. Subjects include AIDS, Anthrax and Bioterrorism, Diphtheria outbreak (1947), material from the Somerville Commission for Women Domestic Violence Taskforce (1995-1998), health legislation, Somerville Community Health Assessments (1992-1994), Somerville Immigrant Providers resource guide, West Nile Virus, and North Metropolitan District Health Officers and Agents conferences and lectures (1947).

Financial Records, 1915-2002 

Scope and Contents

Contains budget documents, ledgers, invoices, and Neighborhood Improvement Grant documents. Records include invoices for goods (a sample), welfare, and medical services including Somerville Contagious Hospital expenses and invoices for medical services from local hospitals and sanatoria.

Arrangement

Records are arranged in five sections: 1. Budget, 1998-2006, 2. Encumbrances, 1938, 3. Invoices, 1941-1948, 4. Ledgers, 1915-1930, 1938, 5. Neighborhood Improvement Grants, 1999-2002.

Related Material note

There are additional budget records in the Board of Health Laurence Howard Executive Clerk series.

Nurse's Reports, 1943-1957 

Scope and Contents

Contains the Public Health Nurse’s reports to the Board of Health of Somerville medical facility statistics. Reports include the number of babies, tuberculosis cases, other communicable disease cases, and information about clinics such as well-baby clinics, x-ray clinics, and inoculation clinics.

Arrangement

Records are arranged chronologically.
