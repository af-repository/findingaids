Historical note

The Board of Health provides inspectional services for food and drink manufacturing and retail, body art and massage therapy practice, animal husbandry, and other ventures requiring health-related oversight.

Return to Table of Contents »

Scope and Contents

Contains licenses, permits, and applications for the manufacture and distribution of food, beverages, and by products; massage, physical therapy, acupuncture, cosmetology, and body modification businesses and practitioners; animal care; and property thermometers. Records also include inspection requests and reports of health and safety ordinance and code violations on Somerville properties.

Ice Cream License and Permit Applications, 1927-1947 

Scope and Contents

Contains applications for licenses to manufacture ice cream and frozen desserts (1927-1935) and applications cards for permits to sell and distribute ice cream and frozen desserts (1947). License applications include information about the business, building, employees, production volume, and source of production materials. Permit applications include information about the business, distribution methods, and the source of the ice cream mix and water.

Arrangement

Records are arranged in two sections: 1. License Applications, 2. Permit Application Cards. Both are arranged chronologically.

Massage Licenses and Applications, circa 1995-2008 

Scope and Contents

Contains licenses and applications for massage therapy, physical therapy, and acupuncture practitioners and businesses. Records include certificates, medical test results, practitioner photographs and resumes, and licensing fee payment records.

Arrangement

Records are arranged in five sections: 1. Massage License Review List, 1999, 2. Massage Licenses, 3. Pending Applications, 4. Non-Renewals, 5. Closed Businesses. Massage Licenses are arranged alphabetically by street name.
