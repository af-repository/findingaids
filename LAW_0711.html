Collection Inventory

Closed Case Files, 1900-1983 

Scope and Contents

Contains records of complaints, claims, petitions, and civil suits brought by or against the City of Somerville. Includes cases pertaining to municipal employment, contractors, welfare, and real estate. There are some Workers' Comp. cases in this section, but most are located in the Closed Workers' Comp. Case Files section. Case file records include summonses, plaintiff and defendant statements, interrogatories, discovery material, medical examination records, settlement records, transcripts, correspondence, audiocassettes, VHS tapes, and photographs. Box 2501-LAW-020 contains property deeds and records of real estate settlements pertaining to properties acquired by the City. A number of cases that were closed in 1973 date from the 1940s-1960s.

Arrangement

Case Files are arranged chronologically by year of case closure and alphabetically by complainant surname or case title within each year. If the date of closure is unknown, then cases are arranged by the last date present in the file.

Processing note

Closed cases dating from 1900-1983 were reappraised for deaccessioning. Many photographs from deaccessioned case files were retained in the Closed Case Photographs series.

Correspondence, 1968-1981 

Scope and Contents

Contains correspondence with City departments and opinions concerning Board of Aldermen orders and ordinances, payroll, Board of Appeals, Board of Assessors, auditing, City services, contracts, Fire Department, Board of Health, Department of Public Works, hiring, licensing and permits, Public Library, Police Department, Comprehensive Employment and Training Act (CETA), and correspondence with the Mayor and Treasurer.

Arrangement

Records are arranged alphabetically by subject.
